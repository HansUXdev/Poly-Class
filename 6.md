# Lesson Plan: Language 101 – May 14, 2025

**Date:** May 14, 2025  
**Lesson #:** 6  
**Unit/Topic:** Describing Daily Activities and What You Are Doing Now  
**Duration:** 50 minutes

---

## Learning Objectives
- Use the present progressive tense to describe what you or others are doing right now.  
- Ask “What are you doing?” and respond with a basic action verb in eight languages.  
- Recognize and pronounce the letter **R** in each script.  
- Identify and pronounce the noun **work/job** in each language.  
- Conjugate and pronounce the verb **to do** or **to work** as applicable in each language.  
- Practice writing and translating vocabulary tables.  
- Create personal Quizlet and Kahoot! sets based on this content.

---

## Standards Alignment
- **CEFR A1:** Can understand and use familiar expressions to describe current actions; can ask what someone is doing and provide basic replies about oneself or others.

---

## Materials (Digital Only)
- Duolingo: “Present Continuous” or “Work” module  
- YouTube: “What Are You Doing Now?” in Multiple Languages  
- Quizlet: student-built flashcard decks  
- Kahoot: student-generated quizzes

---

## Technology Integration
- **Duolingo XP:** 10 XP in “Work” or “Present” units  
- **Quizlet Live:** Matching verbs to present-tense sentences  
- **Kahoot:** Classwide review game using peer-submitted questions  
- **YouTube:** Live-action + animation clips of present-tense examples

---

## Anticipatory Set (5 min)
- Instructor mimes various actions while saying “I am ___ing” in different languages.  
- Students guess actions and the language being spoken.  
- Theme is introduced: talking about what you’re doing *right now*.

---

## Presentation (10 min)
- Watch a YouTube clip showing kids describing what they are doing in different languages.  
- Teacher breaks down sentence structure: subject + present tense of verb + object/complement.  
- Choral repetition of “I am working,” “I am studying,” “What are you doing?” in each language.

---

## Guided Practice (15 min)
- Students pair up and ask each other “What are you doing?” in a target language.  
- Respond with a true or imaginary activity.  
- Switch languages every 2 minutes; instructor rotates for pronunciation support.

---

## Independent Practice (10 min)
1. Copy and translate all four vocabulary tables.  
2. Create and share a Quizlet deck.  
3. Write 5 unique Kahoot! questions based on today’s conversation and vocabulary.

---

## Assessment (5 min)
- One student-created Kahoot! is played live.  
- Exit ticket: Student writes a sentence using “I am ___ing” in three target languages.

---

## Homework
- Duolingo: Complete “Work” and “Present” lessons  
- Quizlet: Practice today's set until 80% mastery  
- YouTube: Watch one video in a new language from today’s set  
- Write a 4-sentence paragraph about your current day using at least 2 languages

## Quizlet Flashcards

1. **Q:** What does “Ich arbeite” mean in English?  
   **A:** I am working  
   **Distractors:** I am reading, I am sleeping, I am eating

2. **Q:** How do you say “job” in Japanese?  
   **A:** 仕事  
   **Distractors:** 学校, 食事, 勉強

3. **Q:** What is “What are you doing?” in Russian?  
   **A:** Что ты делаешь?  
   **Distractors:** Где ты?, Как дела?, Ты студент?

4. **Q:** Translate “I am reading” to Chinese.  
   **A:** 我在读书。  
   **Distractors:** 我在工作。, 我在学习。, 我在吃饭。

5. **Q:** Which letter is “R” in Ukrainian?  
   **A:** Р  
   **Distractors:** П, Б, Д
